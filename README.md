## Introductions

Hello, my name is Lauren and I am a graphic designer learning web development. I am also an artist and originally went to school for studio arts and history of art and architecture. While I have shifted to the design sector, I've brought my keen eye for aesthetic balance and skills in researching, writing, and assessing qualitative information.

I have experience working with Wordpress as well due to our gallery website and shop being hosted through it.

Looking forward, I aim to learn at least basic coding and Figma. UX research fascinates me and I want to learn as much as possible about UX/UI processes and tools. It feels like a natural progression for my combination of graphic design and researching skills.
